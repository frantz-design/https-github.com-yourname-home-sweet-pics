Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can you actually close?

Our average close is 7 days from accepted offer, but we work on your timeline — whether that's 5 days or 5 months.

Do I need to make repairs before selling?

No. We purchase homes completely as-is, including properties with fire, water, structural, or hoarding damage.

Are there any fees or commissions?

None. We cover standard closing costs and there are zero agent commissions — the offer you accept is what you receive.

What types of situations do you help with?

Foreclosure, inherited property, divorce, tired landlords, costly repairs, relocations, and more — if you need to sell, we can help.

Is the cash offer obligation-free?

Completely. There's never any pressure or obligation to accept — review it, ask questions, and decide what's best for you.
